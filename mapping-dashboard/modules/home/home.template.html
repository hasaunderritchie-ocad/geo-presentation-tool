What is Hook?


Hook is a tool that was developed by Hugh Ritchie. It connects users and interested people through data.

The data that is controlled through hook really depends on whomever is hosting a session, or who has brought you here today for their presentation. It can be anything from analytics from a running piece of software, to a map of data about urban planning.
